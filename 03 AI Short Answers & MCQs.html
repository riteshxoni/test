Artificial Intelligence – Exam-Based Short Answers & MCQs

PART 1: Exam-Based Short Answers

1. Define Artificial Intelligence.

AI ka matlab machines ko itna smart banana ki woh insaan jaisa intelligent behavior show kar saken (learning, reasoning, decision-making).

2. What is the goal of AI?

AI ka goal aise intelligent systems banana hai jo tasks automatically perform kare, errors kam kare, aur time ke saath improve hote rahen.

3. Applications of AI.

Healthcare, finance, self-driving cars, education, manufacturing, recommendation systems.

4. What is the Turing Test?

Turing Test check karta hai ki kya machine conversation me human jaisa intelligent behavior dikha sakti hai.

5. What is a Rational Agent?

Rational Agent woh AI system hota hai jo har situation me best possible action choose karta hai taaki goal achieve ho.

6. What is an Intelligent Agent?

Intelligent agent environment ko sense karta hai, decision leta hai aur actuators ke through action perform karta hai.

7. Define Fully Observable Environment.

Jisme agent ko environment ka complete information milta ho — like Chess.

8. Define Partially Observable Environment.

Jisme agent ko incomplete or limited information milti ho — like Driving in fog.

9. What is Deterministic Environment?

Environment jisme next outcome fully predictable hota hai — like puzzles.

10. What is Stochastic Environment?

Environment jisme randomness aur uncertainty hoti hai — like traffic, weather.

PART 2: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) with Answers

1. AI was introduced as a field in:
A. 1943    B. 1956    C. 1965    D. 1972
Answer: B. 1956
2. Turing Test measures:
A. Speed    B. Intelligence    C. Memory    D. Power
Answer: B. Intelligence
3. Which is NOT an AI application?
A. Medical diagnosis    B. Spam filtering    C. Typewriter
Answer: C. Typewriter
4. Rational Agent always chooses:
A. Random action    B. Emotional action    C. Best possible action
Answer: C. Best possible action
5. Intelligent Agent takes action using:
A. Sensors    B. Actuators    C. CPU
Answer: B. Actuators
6. Fully observable environment gives:
A. Partial info    B. Complete info    C. No info
Answer: B. Complete info
7. Driving in fog is an example of:
A. Fully observable    B. Partially observable
Answer: B. Partially observable
8. AI stands for:
A. Automated Input    B. Artificial Intelligence
Answer: B. Artificial Intelligence
9. Machine Learning is a subfield of:
A. Networking    B. AI    C. DBMS
Answer: B. AI
10. Stochastic environment means:
A. No randomness    B. Randomness present
Answer: B. Randomness present
